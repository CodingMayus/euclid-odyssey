Euclid's Odyssey

What's our goal?

The amount of power required to transmit data is synonymous with distance. So transferring planteary seismology research back to Earth requires large amounts of power at a time. However, this process becomes redundant as a only small fraction out of all the continuously recorded data is actually useful. When large amounts of data are being transferred, its resolution will be less optimal, highlighting the necessity for efficient data transmissions. Our goal is to differentiate seismic data from noise such that only useful data is being sent back to Earth.
